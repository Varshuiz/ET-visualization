info About AquaCrop

AquaCrop is FAO's crop water productivity model that simulates yield response to water. It helps farmers and researchers:

  • check_circlePredict crop yields under different water conditions
  • check_circleOptimize irrigation schedules to save water
  • check_circleCompare rainfed vs irrigated production
  • check_circleUnderstand crop water stress and its impact on yield
