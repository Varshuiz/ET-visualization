About AquaCrop

AquaCrop is FAO's crop water productivity model that simulates yield response to water. It helps farmers and researchers:

  • Predict crop yields under different water conditions
  • Optimize irrigation schedules to save water
  • Compare rainfed vs irrigated production
  • Understand crop water stress and its impact on yield
