info About the Maulé Method

Key Features:

  • Minimal meteorological data required
  • Calibrated for Prairie & semi-arid regions
  • Optional humidity correction for better accuracy
  • Ideal when wind data is unavailable

Required Data:

  • Maximum and minimum temperature (°C)
  • Solar radiation (MJ/m²/day)
  • Optional: Relative humidity (%)

Formula:

ET₀ = k × (Tavg + a) × Rs × fRH × fΔT

Where: k = 0.0055, a = 17.8, Rs = incoming solar radiation (MJ/m²/day), fRH = humidity correction (bounded 0.7 to 1.3), and fΔT = temperature-range correction (bounded 0.8 to 1.2).
