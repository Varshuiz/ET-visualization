Maulé Method

Simplified ET estimation calibrated for prairie and semi-arid conditions with minimal data requirements

About the Maulé Method

Key Features:

  • Minimal meteorological data required
  • Calibrated for Prairie & semi-arid regions
  • Optional humidity correction for better accuracy
  • Ideal when wind data is unavailable

Required Data:

  • Maximum and minimum temperature (°C)
  • Solar radiation (MJ/m²/day)
  • Optional: Relative humidity (%)

Formula:

ET₀ = C × Ra × (Tmax - Tmin)^0.5 × (Tavg + 17.8) / 100

Where: C = empirical coefficient, Ra = extraterrestrial radiation, Tmax/Tmin = daily max/min temperature, Tavg = mean daily temperature

Data Requirements

Requires Tmax, Tmin (or Tavg) and solar radiation. Humidity is optional — if provided it improves accuracy.
