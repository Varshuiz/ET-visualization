📚 About the Maulé Method

The Maulé method is a simplified evapotranspiration estimation technique designed for conditions where limited meteorological data is available. It provides reasonable ET estimates using basic temperature and solar radiation parameters, with optional humidity correction.

✓ Minimal Data Requirements

Works with just temperature and solar radiation data

✓ Regional Calibration

Calibrated for prairie and semi-arid conditions

✓ Optional Humidity Correction

Improved accuracy when humidity data is available
