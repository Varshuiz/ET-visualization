📍About ACIS Data

The Alberta Climate Information Service (ACIS) provides high-quality weather data from agricultural weather stations across Alberta. This tool automatically fetches:

  • ✅ Temperature (max/min/average)
  • ✅ Precipitation (daily rainfall)
  • ✅ Relative Humidity (for accurate calculations)
  • ✅ Wind Speed (at 10m height, converted to 2m)
  • ✅ Reference ET (official ACIS calculation for comparison)
