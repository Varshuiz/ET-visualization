Agricultural ET Calculator

Upload your ACIS weather data to calculate evapotranspiration rates for optimal crop management

How to Use

  • Upload CSV files from ACIS (Applied Climate Information System)
  • Ensure your CSV contains columns for Air Temperature (°C) and Solar Radiation (MJ/m²)
  • The tool will calculate ET₀ using the Priestley-Taylor method
  • View results in table format and download processed data
