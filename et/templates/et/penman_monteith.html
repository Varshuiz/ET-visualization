info About Penman-Monteith Method

Key Features:

  • FAO-56 international standard
  • Most accurate ET₀ calculation method
  • Combines energy balance and aerodynamic terms
  • Suitable for all climatic conditions

Required Data:

  • Maximum and minimum temperature (°C)
  • Relative humidity (%)
  • Wind speed at 2m height (m/s)
  • Solar radiation (MJ/m²/day)

Formula:

ET₀ = [0.408Δ(Rn-G) + γ(900/(T+273))u₂(es-ea)] / [Δ + γ(1+0.34u₂)]

Where: Δ = slope of vapor pressure curve, Rn = net radiation, G = soil heat flux, T = mean temperature, u₂ = wind speed, es = saturation vapor pressure, ea = actual vapor pressure
