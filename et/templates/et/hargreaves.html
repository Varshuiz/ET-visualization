Hargreaves-Samani Method

Temperature-only ET estimation — ideal for remote locations and historical periods with limited data

About the Hargreaves-Samani Method

Key Features:

  • Only daily Tmax and Tmin required
  • Validated globally across diverse climates
  • Ideal for remote / data-scarce locations
  • Excellent for long historical records

Required Data:

  • Maximum temperature (°C)
  • Minimum temperature (°C)
  • No radiation, humidity or wind needed

Formula:

ET₀ = 0.0023 × Ra × (Tmax - Tmin)^0.5 × (Tavg + 17.8)

Where: Ra = extraterrestrial radiation (estimated from latitude and day of year), Tmax/Tmin = daily max/min temperature, Tavg = (Tmax + Tmin) / 2

Minimal Data Needed
